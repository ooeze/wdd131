Peni Group Buying Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

Peni Group Buying Hub

This name represents a platform where users can collaborate on group purchases to save money and foster community interaction.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for group buying, offering information about the benefits of group purchases, a community forum for discussions, a sales page for available products, a blog for expert advice, and links to closed social media communities.

Scenarios

  • How can I save money through group buying?
  • What are some success stories of group buying?
  • How do I join the closed Facebook and WhatsApp groups?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Light Blue (#f0f8ff) - Used for backgrounds and accents.
  • Secondary Color: Black (#000000) - Used for text and headings.

Typography

  • Font 1: Arial - Used for body text.
  • Font 2: Georgia - Used for headings.
